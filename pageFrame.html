About the Actionable News Network (A.N.N.)

We believe that news has become something out of our control, and that people should have an idea of what to do in response to the changing times and situations that surround our connected world.

The A.N.N. was created as a way to give control back to people for major news events, with curated recommendations for action

This plugin is a test of this idea, focused on the COVID-19 pandemic of 2019-2020. Feedback is appreciated!
